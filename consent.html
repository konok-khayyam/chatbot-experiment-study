Consent Form

You are invited to participate in a research study conducted by researchers affiliated with TU Wien and Curtin University.

Before deciding whether to participate, please read the information below carefully.

Purpose of the Study

This study examines how people interact with online information and AI-based conversational systems. Participants will answer survey questions, watch a short video, interact with an AI chatbot, and complete follow-up questionnaires.

What Participation Involves

If you choose to participate, you will:

  • Answer survey questions
  • Watch a short video
  • Interact with an AI chatbot
  • Complete follow-up questionnaires

The study will take approximately 20–30 minutes to complete.

AI System Disclosure

During the study, you will interact with an AI chatbot. The chatbot responses are automatically generated by an artificial intelligence system.

Sensitive Content Warning

Some study materials may include controversial political or social claims presented in a fictional or simulated context for research purposes. The study does not endorse these views.

Risks and Discomforts

Some participants may find parts of the study uncomfortable, emotionally challenging, or disagreeable. You may stop participation at any time without penalty.

Data Collected

The study will record:

  • Survey responses
  • Chatbot interaction messages
  • Response timings and interaction metadata related to study participation

Data Storage and Confidentiality

Your responses will be stored securely and analyzed for research purposes only. The researchers will not publish personally identifying information. Data will be anonymized where possible.

Voluntary Participation

Your participation is voluntary. You may stop the study at any time before submitting your responses.

Compensation

You will receive compensation through Prolific according to the study listing and completion requirements.
