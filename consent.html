Consent Form

You are invited to participate in a research study conducted by researchers affiliated with TU Wien and Curtin University.

Please read the participant information below carefully before providing consent.

Purpose of the Study

This study examines how people interact with online information and AI-based conversational systems. Participants will answer survey questions, watch a short video, interact with an AI chatbot, and complete follow-up questionnaires.

What Participation Involves

If you choose to participate, you will:

  • Answer survey questions
  • Watch a short video
  • Interact with an AI chatbot
  • Complete follow-up questionnaires

The study will take approximately 20–30 minutes to complete.

AI System Disclosure

During the study, you will interact with an AI chatbot. The chatbot responses are automatically generated by an artificial intelligence system.

Sensitive Content Warning

Some study materials may include fictional or sanitised examples of polarising, manipulative, or extremist-leaning online content presented in a controlled research context for research purposes. The study does not endorse these views.

Risks and Discomforts

Some participants may find parts of the study uncomfortable, emotionally challenging, or disagreeable. You may stop participation at any time before submitting your responses without penalty. Support information and additional resources will be provided at the end of the study.

Data Collected

The study will record:

  • Survey responses
  • Chatbot interaction messages
  • Response timings and interaction metadata related to study participation

Please do not share directly identifying, highly personal, or sensitive information during chatbot interactions or questionnaire responses.

Data Storage and Confidentiality

Your data will be processed in accordance with applicable data protection regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Research data will be stored securely and accessed only by authorised members of the research team.

Where possible, identifying information will be removed or replaced through anonymisation or pseudonymisation before analysis. Research findings may be published in academic publications or presentations, but no personally identifying information will be included.

If you wish to request withdrawal of submitted data after participation, you may contact the research team. Withdrawal may not be possible after data have been anonymised or aggregated for analysis.

Voluntary Participation

Your participation is voluntary. You may stop the study at any time before submitting your responses.

Compensation

You will receive compensation through Prolific according to the study listing and completion requirements.
