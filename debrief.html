Thank You

Thank you for taking part in this study. This research examined how people respond to online information and AI-based conversational systems.

About the Study Materials

During the study, participants viewed fictional or sanitised examples of polarising or manipulative online content and interacted with an AI chatbot configured for research purposes. The study materials were designed to simulate persuasive forms of online communication that people may encounter in digital environments. The materials were created solely for research purposes and do not reflect the personal views of the researchers or institutions involved.

Chatbot Interaction Conditions

Different participants experienced different versions of the chatbot interaction as part of the experimental design. These versions varied in areas such as conversational style, tone, and communication approach. The purpose of this research is to better understand how chatbot-based responses may support critical reflection and thoughtful engagement with online information.

Support and Withdrawal

Some participants may have experienced mild discomfort or emotional unease while engaging with the study materials. If participation caused discomfort, you are encouraged to take a break and seek support from trusted personal, professional, or local support resources if needed. If you have questions about the study or wish to request withdrawal of your data, you may contact the research team using the information below.
